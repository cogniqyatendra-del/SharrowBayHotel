Our Story

For over 70 years, Sharrow Bay has welcomed guests to one of the most serene corners of the Lake District. Set along Ullswater’s timeless shores, our historic country house blends English heritage with modern understated luxury.

From elegant suites to award-winning dining, our philosophy remains the same: offer warm hospitality, extraordinary culinary experiences, and unforgettable views—ensuring every stay becomes a cherished memory.

Timeless Elegance

There is a quiet beauty in the history that surrounds us. Sharrow Bay is more than just a hotel; it is a living testament to a golden era of hospitality. Every stone, every view, and every moment shared here is steeped in a tradition of grace that has endured for generations.

We invite you to pause, breathe, and immerse yourself in an atmosphere where time seems to slow down. Here, the simple pleasures of life—a stunning view, a glass of fine wine, a warm welcome—are celebrated with an elegance that never goes out of style.

"Simplicity is the keynote of all true elegance."
— Coco Chanel

Our Philosophy

We believe true luxury is found in details—personalized service, warm hospitality, and experiences that stay with you long after your departure. Whether you're here for a peaceful retreat, romantic escape, or fine dining, we welcome you to feel at home.
